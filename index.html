About Us

At Golden Delicious, we celebrate the joy of food through authentic Thai and island-inspired flavors. Founded by Phil and Apple, our culinary journey is rooted in tradition, passion, and a deep love for bringing people together over great meals. We are currently serving delicious meals at Maysville farmers' markets. Every dish reflects our heritage, love for food, and commitment to quality, authenticity, and great company.
